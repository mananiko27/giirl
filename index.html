Welcome to mARTa

This is a place of color, play, memory, and love — a glimpse into handmade joy, creative workshops, and heartfelt connection.

mARTa – girl with a baloon

This space is born from love, devotion, and an eternal bond. It is dedicated to my sweet, blissful, strong-minded, and deeply artistic daughter, Martha — eternally in our hearts, eternally unattainable, eternally 8 years old. She left behind a world of inspiration which lives on in every piece I create.

Her sense of color, shape, and beauty ran deep. Her works echo the purity of thought and the wild strength of imagination.

Every petal and brushstroke she touched bloomed with more than paint — it carried warmth and light like the sunflower itself.

She saw color where the world saw gray. Her rainbows were real — not just arches in the sky, but symbols of feeling.

Inspired yet original, she played with abstraction like a little Picasso, expressing her truth with every angle and swirl.

Nature lived in her — from the fragile curl of a leaf to the strength of a branch. Her soul was rooted, yet free.
