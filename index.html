Welcome to mARTa

This is a place of color, play, memory, and love — a glimpse into handmade joy, creative workshops, and heartfelt connection.

mARTa – girl with a baloon

This space is born from love, devotion, and an eternal bond. It is dedicated to my sweet, blissful, strong-minded, and deeply artistic daughter, Martha — eternally in our hearts, eternally unattainable, eternally 8 years old. She left behind a world of inspiration which lives on in every piece I create.

Her sense of color, shape, and beauty ran deep. Her works echo the purity of thought and the wild stretch of imagination.

Each sunflower she painted radiates her warmth. They grew from the center of her being — joyful, bright, full of light.

The rainbow in her hands was never just color — it was harmony, spirit, and a wish sent skyward.

Her strokes could be whimsical and fierce — raw emotion shaped through vision. Every face she painted had story, every line — a voice.

She once said, “Branches grow from thought.” Her paintings were just that — a bloom from imagination into form.
